Dr. Joy DeGruy

About Joy

Dr. Joy DeGruy is a nationally and internationally renowned researcher, educator, author and presenter. She is an ambassador for healing and a voice for those struggled in search of the past, and continue to struggle through the present. Author of
Post Traumatic Slave Syndrome: Americas Legacy of Enduring Injury and Healing,
Post Traumatic Slave Syndrome: The Study Guide,
with a second book in the works, Post Traumatic Slave Syndrome Part 2: Be The Healing.

Education

Dr. DeGruy holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Communications; two master degrees in Social Work and Clinical Psychology; and a PhD in Social Work Research. With over twenty years of practical experience as a professional in the field of social work, she gives a practical insight into various cultural and ethnic groups that form the basis of contemporary American society.
